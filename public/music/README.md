## Background music (royalty-free)

Put your **properly licensed** background music tracks in this folder.

### Required
- You must have a license that allows **commercial use** and the platforms you publish on (Instagram/TikTok/YouTube).
- Keep a copy of the license text / invoice for each track.

### Expected filenames (default UI options)
- `smooth-01.mp3`
- `smooth-02.mp3`
- `smooth-03.mp3`

You can rename tracks, but then update the mapping in `src/lib/music.ts`.

### Notes
- “Royalty-free” does not always mean “free to use anywhere”. Always check the terms.
- If a track requires attribution, store the attribution text in `src/lib/music.ts` and keep it in your publishing workflow.
